Mac'n'Cheese

Description

This is a great recipe when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Ham
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the crust
  2. Spread crunched tomatoes
  3. Add sheets of ham
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Add a drizzle of oil
